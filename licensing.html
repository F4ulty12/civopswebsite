📜 Licensing & Permits

Civilian Operations regulates licenses, permits, and certifications required for businesses, transportation, construction, and special activities within Kentucky State Roleplay.

🍽️ Food Safety Permit

Required for restaurants and food vendors serving food to the public.

🏥 Health License

Authorizes operation of medical and healthcare-related businesses.

🚧 Public Right Of Way Permit

Allows temporary use of public roads, sidewalks, and government property.

🎪 Special Events Permit

Required for festivals, gatherings, parades, and large public events.

🏗️ Building / Construction Permit

Required before beginning construction, renovation, or major projects.

🏢 Business License

Official authorization to operate a business within the state.

🏦 Bank Charter Authorization

Permits operation of banking institutions and financial services.

💰 Sales Tax Permit

Allows businesses to collect and remit applicable sales taxes.

🔥 Fire / Burn Permit

Required for controlled burns, fire-related events, and demonstrations.

🚛 Commercial Driver License

Certification for operating commercial vehicles and transportation services.

🚗 Driver Permit

Entry-level driving authorization prior to obtaining a full license.
