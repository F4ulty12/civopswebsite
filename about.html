Civilian Operations Department

Welcome to the KSRP Civilian Operations Department. Civ Ops brings the community to life by creating structured, realistic, and immersive civilian roleplay across the state of Kentucky.

What is Civilian Operations?

Civilian Operations oversees all civilian roleplay within Kentucky State Roleplay. It is the foundation of roleplay — without civilians, there is no story or world activity.

Responsibilities
  • Creating and managing civilian roleplay scenarios
  • Operating and maintaining civilian businesses
  • Enforcing realism through state laws and server rules
  • Supporting interaction with LEO, Fire, EMS, and civilians
  • Maintaining fair and immersive roleplay environments
Get Involved

Interested in running a business, hosting events, or contributing to Civ Ops? Contact a Civ Ops Director or open a community support ticket.
