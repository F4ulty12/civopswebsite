🏙 Civilian Operations Department

Welcome to Civ Ops. We manage civilian roleplay across Kentucky State Roleplay, ensuring realism, structure, and immersion.

❓ What is Civ Ops?

Civilian Operations oversees all civilian RP within KSRP and forms the foundation of the server’s world and economy.

📋 Responsibilities
  • Create civilian roleplay scenarios
  • Manage businesses and economy systems
  • Maintain realism and fairness
  • Support all roleplay departments

Join Civ Ops by participating in events, running businesses, or contacting leadership through community channels.
