🏙 Civilian Operations Department

Welcome to the KSRP Civilian Operations Department (Civ Ops). This is where the community brings the state of Kentucky to life through immersive, structured, and realistic roleplay.

❓ What is Civ Ops?

Civilian Operations oversees all civilian roleplay within Kentucky State Roleplay. Without civilians, there is no story, economy, or immersion.

📋 Responsibilities
  • Create and manage civilian RP scenarios
  • Operate civilian-owned businesses
  • Enforce realism through rules and laws
  • Support LEO, Fire, EMS, and civilians
  • Maintain fair and immersive gameplay
🤝 Get Involved

Join Civ Ops by running businesses, hosting events, or contributing ideas. Contact a Civ Ops Director or open a community ticket.
