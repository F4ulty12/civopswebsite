:CO: | Civilian Operations

Welcome to the KSRP Civilian Operations Department (Civ Ops). This is where you, the community, bring life to the state of Kentucky. Civ Ops ensures realistic, enjoyable roleplay while balancing fairness, creativity, and immersion for everyone.

:Question_mark1: | What is Civ Ops?

Civilian Operations oversees all civilian roleplay within Kentucky State Roleplay. Civ Ops is the foundation of RP — without civilians, there’s no story.

:clipboard: | Responsibilities of Civ Ops
  • Creating and maintaining civilian roleplay scenarios
  • Operating and managing civilian-owned businesses
  • Following KSRP server rules and state laws
  • Providing creative, realistic interactions for LEO, Fire/EMS, and other civilians
  • Ensuring civilian RP is fair, balanced, and immersive
:handshake: | Get Involved

Interested in running a business, planning events, or helping Civ Ops grow? Reach out through a Community Ticket or speak with a Civ Ops Director.
